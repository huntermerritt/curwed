Hannah & Hunter

The story of us.

Hannah and Hunter grew up in the same town of Barrington, IL and were introduced to each other freshman year of highschool. It wasn’t until sophomore year that they started seeing a lot of eachother. While Hannah was at cross country practice, she ran laps around the football field Hunter was practicing on, she sat in front of Hunter in Honors Trigonometry, where he constantly made her laugh before class started and in between the teacher’s explanations, and they went to the same high school ministry at Willow Creek Community Church. Hannah would go over to Hunter’s lunch table to ask her friend cross country friend a question that she already knew the answer to, and then the next day Hunter would go over to Hannah’s lunch table to ask her for math help even though he didn’t really need it. On September 25th, 2014 Hannah asked Hunter out and the rest is kind of history! They sat through spanish class together, cheered each other on at track meets, danced the night away at Homecoming, sang their favorite songs on repeat in the car, spent summers at the bowling alley, and decided to go to Texas Christian University together starting Fall 2016. On May 28th, 2018 on the back deck of a cruise ship traveling to Alaska, Hunter got down on one knee and asked Hannah to marry him, and she said YES!! After the engagement, they went back to TCU where Hunter is finishing up his last two semesters before graduating and working at AT&T in Dallas full time as a software developer, and Hannah is still working on her junior year, pursuing her BFA in Art Education.
